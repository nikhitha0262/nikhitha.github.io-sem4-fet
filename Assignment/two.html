The Future of Technology

Introduction

Technology is growing very quickly. New tools and systems are changing how people work, communicate, and solve problems in everyday life.

Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence (AI) helps computers learn from data and perform smart tasks. Many industries use AI to improve services and make better decisions.

Cloud Computing

Cloud computing lets people store data and use applications through the internet. It helps businesses save money and work more efficiently.

Conclusion

Technology will continue to shape the future. Learning new technologies will help people stay updated in a fast-changing world.
